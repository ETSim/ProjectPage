Simulating friction, wear, and surface interactions using advanced computational techniques

Project Abstract

This research presents a novel approach to simulating friction and surface interactions through computer-aided fabrication and validation of 3D surfaces. By leveraging high-resolution scanning techniques, advanced computational models, and experimental validation, the project develops a comprehensive methodology for understanding friction behavior across multiple scales.
