Harnessing Gelsight scan textures to simulate complex friction, wear, and lubrication phenomena in the texture space.

Project Abstract

This project presents a novel simulator that leverages high-resolution textures from Gelsight scans to model friction, wear, and lubrication behavior directly in the texture space. By integrating detailed surface information, our simulation captures nuanced interactions between contact surfaces, providing insights into material behavior under various conditions. The approach paves the way for enhanced predictions in material science and engineering applications.
